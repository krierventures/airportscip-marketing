Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AIRPORTSCIP LLC ("AirportsCIP," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the AirportsCIP platform at airportscip.com and app.airportscip.com (the "Service"). The Service is a business tool used by airports and their representatives; it is not directed to consumers or children.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account and contact information — such as name, email address, job title, organization, and phone number for you and the users you invite.
  • Content you enter into the Service — airport, project, phase, grant, funding, financial, vendor, task, and compliance information, and documents you upload (for example, award letters or supporting files). This is "Customer Data."
  • Communications — messages you send us (support requests, "Ask an Expert" questions, demo or contact form submissions).
  • Billing information — if you subscribe to a paid plan, we send you an invoice and you pay by check or direct deposit (ACH). We do not collect or store payment card numbers.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage and device data — such as log data, IP address, browser type, pages viewed, actions taken, timestamps, and similar analytics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — used to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and operate and improve the Service. See "Cookies" below.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • provide, operate, secure, maintain, and improve the Service;
  • create and manage accounts and authenticate users;
  • generate the features you request (including AI-assisted drafts, summaries, and document parsing — see "AI Processing");
  • communicate with you about your account, support requests, security, and Service updates;
  • manage invoicing and subscriptions;
  • monitor for, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security incidents, and violations of our Terms; and
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

3. AI Processing

Certain features use third-party artificial-intelligence providers to generate drafts, summaries, form pre-fills, and parsed data. When you use these features, the relevant inputs (which may include Customer Data you choose to submit) are transmitted to and processed by our AI provider(s) to produce the output. We use AI providers under business/commercial terms intended to keep your data confidential, used only to provide the feature, and not used to train the providers' models. AI output may be inaccurate; you are responsible for reviewing it (see our Terms of Service).

4. How We Share Information

We share information only as described here:

  • Service providers / subprocessors — vendors that host and operate the Service on our behalf. They are permitted to use the information only to provide services to us. Our current subprocessors are:
    • Supabase — database hosting and user authentication
    • Vercel — application/website hosting
    • Render — backend/API hosting
    • Anthropic — AI processing for AI-assisted features
    • Resend — transactional and notification email delivery
    • Cal.com — demo and meeting scheduling

  • Legal and safety — when we believe disclosure is required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of AirportsCIP, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.
  • With your direction or consent — when you ask us to share information or otherwise consent.

We may also create and use aggregated or de-identified information that does not identify you or any individual for any lawful purpose.
